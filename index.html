XAVIER WEVERS

My name is Xavier Wevers and I specialize myself in the making of games, I do this using Unity and C#. I am currently studying Software-Development at SintLucas Eindhoven in the Netherlands. Before I started developing Unity games, I was known as a YouTube content creator designing games inside of Fortnite. During the Covid-19 pandemic my channel reached its golden age. I had millions of players enjoying my games in Fortnite. I loved the feeling, having people play with what I made! This was a big factor for me why I chose to start with actual game development.
